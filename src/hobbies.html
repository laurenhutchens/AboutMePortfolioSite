Why I Love My Hobbies

I am a lover of writing, from journals to programming. When I'm not typing away, you'll find me with a pen in my right hand, jotting down aspirations, observations, and resolutions.

The home of my journaling lives in my beautiful bedroom, where the walls are covered in movie and show posters I adore.

Watching shows and movies has taught me lessons that have shaped my personality. The Vampire Diaries, Gilmore Girls, Twilight, Pretty Little Liars, and Harry Potter are not only all over my walls, but also deeply woven into who I am.

Why These Shows?

Each show I love has taught me something:

  • Vampire Diaries taught loyalty.
  • Gilmore Girls taught independence.
  • Harry Potter sparked my imagination.

These passions may seem small, but they’ve taught me discipline, creativity, and perspective—qualities I bring with me into everything I do, from academics to my career.
